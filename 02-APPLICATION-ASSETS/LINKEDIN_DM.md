# LinkedIn DM Templates (Short + Human)

## Recruiter DM
Hi <Name>, I’m a React/MERN developer (Dhaka, open to remote).  
I built <Project> with <proof>. If you’re hiring for <Role>, may I share links?

## Hiring manager DM
Hi <Name>, I enjoyed <something specific about company/product>.  
I’m applying for <Role>. I built <Project> (link) with <relevant proof>.  
Would love 10 minutes for feedback or next steps.

## Don’ts
- Don’t paste huge paragraphs
- Don’t attach 10 links
- Don’t ask “Any update?” without context
