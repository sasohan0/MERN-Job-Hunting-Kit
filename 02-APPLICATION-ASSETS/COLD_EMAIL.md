# Cold Email + Follow-up Templates (Recruiter / Hiring Manager)

## Golden rule
Short. Proof-first. One clear ask.

## Email 1 (50–120 words)
Subject: <Role> — <Your Name> (React/MERN)

Hi <Name>,  
I’m a React/MERN developer. I built <Project> with <proof aligned to role>.  
If you’re hiring for <Role>, may I share my resume + project links?

Resume: <link> | Portfolio: <link> | GitHub: <link> | Best project: <link>  
Thanks, <Name> | <Phone>

## Follow-up 1 (Day 2)
Hi <Name>, just following up on my <Role> application.  
One relevant proof: <1 line>. Happy to share details. Thanks!

## Follow-up 2 (Day 5)
Hi <Name>, last follow-up from my side. If it’s not a fit now, no worries.  
Would you suggest who I should contact for <Role>? Thanks!
